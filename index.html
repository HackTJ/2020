May 16-17, 2015
Thanks to everyone who made HackTJ 2.0 a success!

On May 16-17, 2015, almost 300 high school students from around the DC area gathered at Thomas Jefferson High School for Science and Technology for a weekend of learning, teamwork, and coding. With the help of our mentors, sponsors, and a little caffeine, those students built more than 60 mobile apps, websites, and other projects with their own creativity and ingenuity.

Over 400 photos have been posted on Facebook - take a look to relive the weekend!
